Benchmark round: IssueCertificate

S4 Hybrid-Batch — Issue 10 certificates per Fabric transaction. Linear ramp 50→200 TPS over 60 s (8 workers). Hash: BLAKE3(SHA-256(fields)) per certificate. MVCC-safe: no GetState inside write loop. Effective cert throughput at 200 TPS = 4,000 certs/sec.

  opts:
