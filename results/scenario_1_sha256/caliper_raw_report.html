Benchmark round: VerifyCertificate

S1 SHA-256 — Linear-rate 500→1500 TPS verify (CPU-bound stress test). SHA-256 re-hash × 100 iterations per verify: 100 × 15µs = 1500µs CPU/tx. At 1500 TPS: 1500 × 1500µs = 2,250,000µs/s CPU demand on peer → saturation. This is the KEY round where SHA-256 sequential bottleneck becomes visible. 

txDuration: 60
rateControl:
  type: linear-rate
  opts:
    startingTps: 50
    finishingTps: 300
