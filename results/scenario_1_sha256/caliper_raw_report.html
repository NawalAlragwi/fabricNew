Benchmark round: IssueCertificate

S1 SHA-256 — Issue 1,200 certs at 20 TPS fixed-rate, 60s. 5KB payload: SHA-256 processes ~5,100 bytes x1000 = 15,000 us overhead/tx. 20 TPS chosen to stay within WSL2/CouchDB safe write throughput. contractId: bcms-sha256 (FIX-CONTRACTID).

Benchmark round: VerifyCertificate

S1 SHA-256 — LINEAR-RATE 50→150 TPS verify (60s). PRIMARY COMPARISON ROUND: VerifyCertificateByID re-computes SHA-256 on-chain on every call: 15,000 us CPU overhead per transaction. i7-1255U P-cores saturate earlier than BLAKE3 (AVX2 vs no-SIMD). Expected: SHA-256 latency increases steeply above 100 TPS. contractId: bcms-sha256 (FIX-CONTRACTID). 

txDuration: 60
rateControl:
  type: linear-rate
  opts:
    startingTps: 50
    finishingTps: 300
