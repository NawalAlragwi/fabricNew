Benchmark round: VerifyCertificate

S2 BLAKE3 — LINEAR-RATE 20→100 TPS verify (60s). PRIMARY COMPARISON ROUND: BLAKE3 re-hash = 6,015 us/tx (vs SHA-256: 22,500 us/tx). i7-1255U AVX2: P-core handles 4 BLAKE3 lanes simultaneously. Expected: BLAKE3 latency stays low while SHA-256 saturates P-cores above 100 TPS. This is where T2 < T1 is proven. Caliper will show lower avg latency and higher throughput for BLAKE3 vs SHA-256 in this round. contractId: bcms-blake3 (FIX-CONTRACTID — CRITICAL).

rateControl:
  type: linear-rate
  opts:
    startingTps: 20
    finishingTps: 100
