Benchmark round: VerifyCertificate

S2 BLAKE3 — Linear-rate 500→1500 TPS verify (CPU-bound stress test). BLAKE3 re-hash × 100 iterations per verify: 100 × 4µs = 400µs CPU/tx. At 1500 TPS: 1500 × 400µs = 600,000µs/s CPU demand → 3.75x less than SHA-256. SIMD acceleration (AVX2) gives BLAKE3 clear advantage — latency stays low. 

txDuration: 60
rateControl:
  type: linear-rate
  opts:
    startingTps: 50
    finishingTps: 600
